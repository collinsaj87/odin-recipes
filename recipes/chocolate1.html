Old Fashioned Choctail

Description

This old time classic cocktail also packs excellent flavours for a chocolate bonbon. Perfect for the whiskey connoissieur and chocoholic alike!

Ingredients
  • 310g sugar
  • 90g Glucose Syrup
  • 30g Sorbitol
  • 300g Double Cream
  • 80g Skimmed Milk
  • 1g Sodium Bicarbonate
  • 5g Vanilla
  • 45g Butter
  • 20g Cocoa butter
  • 120g of your favourite Bourbon
Steps
  1. Mix cream, milk, vanilla, sodium and sorbitol and warm to approx. 80C.
  2. Meanwhile heat an empty saucepan and add sugar and glucose. Continue to heat until you get an amber-coloured caramel.
  3. Gradually add the warm cream mixture (step 1) and mix well.
  4. Add butter and heat to 118C
  5. Take the caramel off heat and add cocoa butter, mixiing well.
  6. Cool to below 75C, add Bourbon and mix well.
